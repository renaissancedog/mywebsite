About Us

Welcome to Renaissance Dog Walkers! Our team of experienced and trained dog walkers is dedicated to providing dogs with the exercise, attention, and care they need. We believe in promoting an active lifestyle for dogs and prioritize their health and well-being. Our team consists of four employees who are passionate about providing quality services to our clients. We look forward to hearing from you and providing your dog with the care and attention they deserve!

Please note that we specialize in dog services and are unable to take care of any other type of pet. Currently, we only operate within the Renaissance neighborhood. If you have any questions or would like to learn more about our services, please don't hesitate to contact us at renaissancedog86@gmail.com or through our Google form.
